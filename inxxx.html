Krishna Kumar Kunnath(1968-2022)

Noted Bollywood Legendary Singer

KK was a legendary bollywood singer.A singer who sang more than 500 songs in multiple languages,more than 5000 jingles for various advertisement companies.As a singer he was known for his versatility, for his soulful singing and crystal clear voice.He recorded songs in Hindi,Engish, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Marathi, Odia, Bengali, Assamese and Gujarati.He was a prominent Singer of Hindi film industry.
